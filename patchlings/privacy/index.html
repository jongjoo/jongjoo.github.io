Patchlings Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

App Summary

Patchlings is an offline solo territory-capture game. The app does not require an account, does not use a game server, and stores gameplay progress on your device.

Data Stored On Device

The app stores gameplay progress, score, unlocked characters, medals, quests, best scores, and settings locally on your device. This local progress is not sent to our server.

Data We Do Not Collect Directly

The app does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, payment information, account credentials, or user-generated content.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or receive data such as IP address, device identifiers, advertising data, app interaction data, crash logs, and performance data. This data may be used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, SDK performance, and ad delivery.

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the app may ask for App Tracking Transparency permission before requesting personalized advertising identifiers. If you deny or skip this request, the game remains playable and ads may still be shown in a less personalized form.

Children

Patchlings is not classified as a child-directed app. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Privacy Choices

The app includes Google User Messaging Platform hooks to check advertising consent status before ad requests and to show privacy options when required in supported regions.
