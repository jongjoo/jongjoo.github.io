Service

Patchlings is an offline solo mobile game. The app provides local gameplay, local progression, unlockable characters, medals, quests, and advertising-supported rewards.

The launch version does not provide an account system, cloud save, multiplayer server, or cross-device synchronization. If the app is deleted or device data is cleared, local progress may be lost.

The app may show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. Rewarded ad benefits are granted only when the ad network reports that a reward was earned. Ads may be unavailable because of network conditions, region, ad inventory, device settings, consent status, or SDK errors.

Purchases

The launch version does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Fair Use

Users should not modify, reverse engineer, abuse, or interfere with the app, ad systems, or platform services. Local-only progress can be affected by device time changes, deletion, reinstall, or operating system behavior.

Changes

The app may change gameplay balance, ads, rewards, characters, stages, or supported regions in future versions.
