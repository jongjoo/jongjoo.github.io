Privacy Policy for 로또 AI 추첨

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Overview

로또 AI 추첨 is a personal iOS application committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collected

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, IP address, crash logs, performance data, and other app interaction data for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, SDK performance, and ad delivery.

Information Not Collected Directly

The app does not directly collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, financial information, or account credentials.

Data Storage

App data is stored locally on your device unless a feature clearly indicates otherwise. Ad-related data is managed by Google AdMob according to Google's privacy practices.

Your Choices

You can control tracking and personalized advertising in iOS Settings under Privacy & Security.

Third-Party Services

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use Google services.
